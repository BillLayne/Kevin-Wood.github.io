Got Questions?

We've Got Answers.

What are my liability limits and are they enough?

Your quote includes liability limits of $50,000 for bodily injury per person, $100,000 for bodily injury per accident, and $50,000 for property damage. This provides significantly more protection than the NC state minimums of $30k/$60k/$25k. It's a solid level of coverage for protecting your assets in case of an at-fault accident.

What is "Other Than Collision" and do I need it?

This coverage, also known as Comprehensive, is very valuable. It pays for damage to your car from events *other than* an accident, like theft, vandalism, fire, hail, or hitting an animal. Since you have it on all three vehicles, you're protected from a wide range of unexpected incidents. You chose a low $100 deductible, which is excellent!

Why is the EFT payment plan cheaper?

Progressive offers a discount for using Automatic Payments from a bank account (EFT) because it's a more secure and reliable payment method for them. This results in a lower installment fee per payment ($1.00 for EFT vs. $3.00 for Card/Mail), which we pass on as savings directly to you over the course of the policy.
